WHAT IS RACIAL EQUALITY?

Racial equality is a situation in which people of all races are treated in the same way. In other words, regardless of physical traits such as skin color, racial equality occurs when institutions give individuals legal, moral, and political rights.

WHY DOES IT MATTER?

Racial justice and equality goes beyond simply dispelling racial discrimination and disparities through kinder words or actions. It is also about increasing the amount of supports and policies in our system that are aimed at achieving and sustaining racial justice and equality. By being informed, you can be part of the path to ensuring racial equality is in place.
